Who Is Alexis Floyd? Her Boyfriend, Height, Net Worth

July 2024 · 5 minute read

Nearly two decades after Meredith, Cristina, Alex, Izzie, and George were introduced to Grey’s Anatomy views, there’s again a new class of misfit interns–which includes Mika Yasuda (Midori Francis), Simone Griffith (Alexis Floyd), and Benson “Blue” Kwan (Harry Shum Jr.)–in the 19th season of the popular ABC show. Below is an article on Alexis Floyd, who recently became a series regular on Grey’s Anatomy.

Alexis Floyd On Grey’s Anatomy

Viewers were introduced to Alexis’ character Simone Griffith, along with other interns such as Mika Yasuda and Benson “Blue” Kwan, on the Season 19 premiere (titled “Everything Has Changed) of Grey’s Anatomy. Despite the episode’s title “Everything Has Changed,” many things are pretty much the same between the new intern class and the OG class of 2005.

Alexis, who shot to popularity with Netflix’s Inventing Anna, shared with People that she was blown away when she found out she was accepted for the show.

To her Instagram followers, Alexis shared the news only on July 20, 2022. She uploaded the screenshots of Deadline’s article headlined “Grey’s Anatomy Adds ‘Inventing Anna’s Alexis Floyd As New Series Regular For Season 19.”

Talking about her character Dr. Simone Griffin, Alexis told that she finds her character “a hard worker,” who has “a personal, as well as sort of a political, drive to her work in the hospital.” Although it’s a challenging place for her to be, Alexis shared that it’s also the place that invigorates Simone, a place “to not only empower her as a physician” but also “be a place of foundational change in the field of medicine.”

Adding to that, Alexis said, “She’s definitely very focused…You’ll see her grow and change… You’re going to see her grow as a person, as a doctor, and see how those things inner work which is a special thing for her.”

What Movies And TV Shows Was Alexis Floyd In?

Born in a musical family, Alexis took up the violin at the age of 3. As a child, she did violin recitals at the Youth Orchestra, studied at the Cleveland School of Ballet, and participated in musical theatre at several venues.

Alexis completed her high school studies at Hawken School and then went on to Carnegie Mellon to study musical theater. In 2012, she was at The Rhodopi International Theater Collective in Bulgaria, where she studied the Suzuki method, mime, and aerial art.

Initially in her acting career, Alexis worked at the off-Broadway theatres. Some of her theater credits include Once On This Island, Flakes, Hairspray, Footloose, An Inspector Calls, Wanderlust, The Wiz, Rent, Caroline, or Change, and Ohio Premiere of Thirteen, among others.

In 2019,  Alexis landed the recurring role of Tia Clayton in the TV series The Bold Type.

The Grey’s Anatomy actress got her big break when she landed the role of Neff, in Netflix’s Inventing Anna. At the time of her audition for the role of Neff, she was doing the front-desk job at a high-end yoga studio in New York. Alexis’ portrayal of Neff was critically well received, with some even describing it as a “scene stealer and truly magnetic.

When asked about what pushed her to become an actress, she shared with Schon Magazine that the commitment to the craft of acting happened to her when she did her first musical, The Wiz, at East Cleveland Community Theatre. Alexis recalled the feeling of rooting into the ground that happened after her first costume fitting.

Besides acting, Alexis also does choreography and dancing on music videos. Some of her dance credits include Gimme All Your Love, Oxford Comma, and ETA. Furthermore, Inventing Anna actor recently released her single, Young Sunday, which is now available for streaming.

Alexis Floyd Family

Talking about Alexis’ family, she was born to her parents, Mark Floyd and Lauren Generette.

While Alexis’ mother was surprisingly silent on Alexis’ Grey’s Anatomy stint, her father Mark Floyd wrote, “It is now official! This is my daughter Alexis’ new project… She will be on Season 19 of Grey’s Anatomy as the lead character,” on his IG on July 20, 2022.

Alexis’ father Mark Floyd worked as a labor and employment lawyer as of 2022 whereas her mother, Lauren, was the Director of Instrumental Pathways Programs at The Cleveland Orchestra.

Alexis has one sibling, a brother named Aric Floyd. He is also an actor by profession and has worked on series like Days of Our Lives, Ballers, and Versus.
