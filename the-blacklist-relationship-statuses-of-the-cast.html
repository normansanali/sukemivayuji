The Blacklist: Relationship Statuses Of The Cast

September 2024 · 5 minute read

The characters on Blacklist are played by some of the most interesting actors around! When the first episode premiered back in 2013, its focus on criminal activity, mobsters, spies, and the FBI was instantly enticing.

RELATED: 10 Things The Cast Of The Blacklist Has Said About Working On The Hit Show

The crime TV series has one for eight seasons so far with fans continually hoping for more! The cast of the show deals with on-screen relationships all the time as true fans of the show already know-- but the real-life relationships that the cast is involved in behind the scenes are sometimes considered even more interesting.

10 Megan Boone Is Dating Dan Estabrook

Megan plays an FBI agent and profiler named Elizabeth Keen on Blacklist. She was also a regular actress on other crime-focused TV shows like Law & Order and Blue Bloods back in the day. In real life, Megan leaves the law enforcement stuff to the professionals and sticks to modeling and acting. She has been in a relationship with Dan Estabrook since 2016 and they share one child together -- Caroline. Fun fact about Megan Boone? She was the first person to be officially cast on the hit TV show back in 2013before all the other actors were chosen -- even James Spader.

9 James Spader Is Dating Leslie Stefanson

James Spader has been dating Leslie Stefanson since 2002 and although they haven’t tied the knot, James has been married before! Between 1987 and 2004, he was married to Victoria Spader. He shares three kids with his ex-wife: Sebastian, Elijah, and Nathaneal. Who remembers James’ brief stint on The Office? He played the no-nonsense role of Robert California! These days he’s playing Raymond Reddington on Blacklist. Back in 2014, Rolling Stone labeled him the “strangest man on TV” which says a lot to anyone who might be curious about what it’s like to spend a day with him. He is known for being a super intense man who takes on quirky, oddball Hollywood roles. 

8 Clark Middleton Is Married To Elissa Middleton

The character of Glen Carter is played by none other than Clark Middleton. In real life, he is married to Elissa Middleton. They have been married since 2006 which is a very big deal. That is a long-lasting relationship to have! People all over the world hope and pray for long-lasting relationships like that one.

7 Diego Klattenhoff Is Married To An Unknown Woman

Diego Klattenhoff once worked as a bartender to make ends meet before getting his big Hollywood break. Nowadays, people recognize him more than ever as FBI Agent Donald Ressler. Unfortunately for his fans, what we know about his fictional character persona is so much more than we know about his personal life. On the show, we saw him have a relationship with Audrey Bidwell before she passed away. In real life, Diego is married but… we don’t know anything about his wife! We don’t even know what her name is!

6 Amir Arison Dating An Unknown Woman

Amir Arison is another cast member from Blacklist. He posts a lot of pictures of his dog on social media but none of his girlfriend. He did say, "My girlfriend gets jealous when I call [my dog] ‘baby girl.’ Because my girlfriend wants to be called baby girl. So now I call them my baby girls." Whoever his girlfriend is, at least she gets to be called the nickname that she likes!

5 Mozhan Marnò Is Single

As far as romantic partners go, Mozhan Marnò is living a free and single life without connections to anyone in that way. On Blacklist, she plays the role of Samar Navabi and she definitely adds to the overall amazingness of the show. It will be interesting to uncover who she decides to date next.

4 Hisham Tawfiq Is Married To Spirit Tawfiq

Hisham Tawfiq is known for his role as a security detail man, Dembe Zuma. The character he plays is resourceful, intelligent, and cunning. In real life, he is married to Spirit Tawfiq and they share a son. Just like Diego Klattenhoff, Hisham doesn’t really like to let the world in on his personal life -- and that includes details about his family.

He is rarely ever photographed with his wife or son. Hisham doesn’t just limit himself to acting, either. He used to be a professional dancer, firefighter, and at one point, he was in the Marines. His passion for acting has been a lifelong dream of his and before getting on TV, he starred in a theater production of ‘A Raisin in the Sun’, a life-changing and mega-inspiring play.

3 Harry Lennix Is Married To Djena Nichole Graves

Harry Lennix plays the role of Harold Cooper on the show. He does a great job on TV and a great job in real life too obviously since he has been married for so many years. He and Djena Nichole Graves tied the knot in 2009 and are still going strong to this day. Good for them!

2 Ryan Eggold Is Single

Ryan Eggold might play a married man on Blacklist but in real life, he is as single as they come. On the show, he plays Tom Keene, married to Elizabeth Keene. In real life, he’s been pretty open and honest about his relationship status.

When asked if he was engaged to any women on the Today Show in 2018, he responded by saying, “No, I’m not engaged… who said that? I am the same old broken alone man that you met from last time.” Although those words might sound a tad depressing, it seems like he’s taking single life all in stride and, more than anything, playing with sarcasm to lighten the mood on the subject.

1 Parminder Nagra Is Single

Parminder Nagra is another great actress to come from Blacklist. It's no surprise that the show has been so successful thus far. In her personal life, Nagra was married to James Stenson from 2009 to 2013. He is known for being a great photographer. The relationship did not last and now she is living a single life.
