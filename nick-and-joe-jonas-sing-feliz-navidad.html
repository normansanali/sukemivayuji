Nick and Joe Jonas Sing &quot;Feliz Navidad&quot;

July 2024 · 1 minute read

Jingle Ball tour 2015 ended with a bang as 2/3 of the Jonas Brothers had a mini reunion to sing a holiday classic.

93.3 FLZ’s Jingle Ball in Tampa was star studded: 5 Seconds of Summer, Shawn Mendes, Demi Lovato, Zedd, literally everyone who had a hit in 2015 was there!

Jingle Ball is rather infamous for star mash ups and Tampa did not disappoint. Tove Lo joined Demi Lovato on stage to sing her jam “Cool For The Summer”. Then in a sudden moment our 2008 hearts filled with joy as Mr. Nick Jonas invited his brother and former band mate’s new group DNCE to join him on stage. Only DNCE’s leader singer, no other than Joe Jonas popped along for an acoustic cover of “Feliz Navidad”. Note: The Jonas boys have admitted multiple times they do not know Spanish.

Check it out for yourself

But Joe was not done, he earlier in the show crashed 5 Seconds of Summer’s set to what else D-A-N-C-E! The two groups have been getting along awfully well, sparking hopes that that Jonas’ group DNCE will be the opening act for 5SOS’ Sounds Live Feels Live World Tour which starts in February. (We’re so in!)
