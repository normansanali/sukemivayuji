David Njoku was born in Cedar Grove, NJ on July 10, 1996. Athletic tight end from the University of Miami who was the 3rd 1st round pick of the Cleveland Browns in the 2017 NFL Draft. Fellow Cleveland 1st round picks included Myles Garrett and Jabrill Peppers. He and OJ Howard were 2 of the top tight ends in the stellar tight end class of the 2017 NFL Draft.
