Who Are The Levy Family?

Over the last several years, it has become pretty clear that a lot of people throw around the word legend far too freely. However, even though Eugene Levy has never been the kind of star that headlines blockbuster movies, there is little doubt that he is a comedy legend. First introduced to the world due to his role starring in the sketch comedy series SCTV, Eugene also worked as a writer on the popular show. In the years since that show came to an end, Eugene has gone on to star in endless movies including the American Pie series and Christopher Guest’s various mockumentaries.

Even though the people closest to her have become famous, little is known about the Levy family matriarch Deborah Divine. One thing that is very clear about Divine though is that she is very proud of her family and before she closed her Twitter account, she was an outspoken supporter of the LGBTQ+ community.

In the years since Schitt’s Creek debuted on television, Dan Levy has become one of the most successful voices of his generation in Hollywood. Best known for starring in, co-creating, and working as the showrunner for Schitt’s Creek, Dan has also begun to branch out. Set to be the creative force behind many upcoming projects, Dan also became a movie star thanks to his role in Happiest Season.

A decade before Schitt’s Creek premiered on television, Sarah Levy made her screen debut with a small part in the movie Cheaper by the Dozen 2. In the years that followed that initial role, Sarah popped up in the movie Larry Crowne and a few mostly unnoticed projects. Now that Sarah has proven how wonderful she was as a part of Schitt’s Creek core cast, fans everywhere hope that her career will only go up from here.

Is Sarah Levy Jealous Of Eugene Levy And Dan Levy’s Relationship?

In an ideal world, every parent with multiple kids would have an equal bond with all of their children. In reality, however, the truth of the matter is that most parents in that situation wind up having a little bit more of a bond with one child above the rest for a variety of reasons. Of course, that isn’t to say that parents with a favorite kid love their other children any less. Instead, it is just an acceptance of the reality of human relationships.

Of course, it should go without saying that making assumptions about the Levy family from the outside looking in would be ridiculous. That said, anyone with a basic understanding of people will know that there is a good chance that Eugene Levy and Dan Levy have an incredibly close bond. After all, there aren’t too many people who know what it is like to co-create and star in a hit show. Since Dan and Eugene did that together, having that in common almost certainly brought them closer together.

Despite the assumptions that can be made about Dan Levy and Eugene Levy’s relationship, there is absolutely no indication that Sarah Levy feels like someone on the outside looking in. On the contrary, when Sarah has spoken about her brother and father, it has seemed very clear that she adores them both and in at least one way, she is glad to not be in their position. After all, when she spoke to Flare in 2017, Sarah stated that she was relieved to not be wrapped up in the attention Dan and Eugene receive as a pair.

“When the show first came out, everyone assumed I was the sister. I love that that wasn’t the case because it has fully removed me from the hubbub of Daniel and Dad being father and son onscreen. I loved that people didn’t know that I was related in any way, because I was able to do my own thing.”

On top of what Sarah Levy has said about not envying the attention that her father and brother receive together, something that Dan Levy once revealed made it clear she has no reason to be jealous. After all, when Dan appeared on Jimmy Kimmel Live! and talked about his sister’s 2021 wedding, it became clear that both of Sarah’s parents care deeply about her. After all, Eugene and Deborah Divine worked out a duet and surprised Sarah by performing at her wedding reception. Since some parents are reluctant to even speak at their child’s wedding, that says a lot.
