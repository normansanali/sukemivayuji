Who is Tieghan Gerard?

Tieghan Gerard is a food photographer & blogger, stylist, recipe developer, self-taught cook, and also a cookbook author.

Her blog, Half Baked Harvest is the best food blog emphasizing her savory, sweet, healthy, and indulgent recipes.

Tieghan Gerard: Age, Parent, Childhood, Ethnicity

Tieghan was born on  September 15, 1993, in Ohio, U.S. She spent her childhood in Cleveland, Ohio, and moved to Breckenridge, Vail, Colorado with family at the age of 14. Her ancestry is Anglo-Normans.

Her parents are Jen Gerad and Conard Gerard.  They work in a dairy barn at the bottom of the backyard terrain park. Their barn was later renovated for Tieghan’s business.

Tieghan’s mother also runs the business part of Half Baked Harvest, from sponsorships to bookkeeping. She credits her mom for the blog’s success and for completing the renovation.

The vlogger has six siblings: three elder brothers, two younger brothers, and a younger sister, Asher of 11 yrs.

Before, Tieghan had plans to go to the fashion design school and dream of being a stylist. Even she went to LA and join fashion classes before high school.

But she was homesick and later returned to her home. She develops an interest in cooking and preparing dishes for her family.

Education

Tieghan completed her high school education in her hometown.

And she has college courses through the community college in Colorado. She graduated and has an associate’s degree.

Tieghan Gerard: Career, Professional life

Tieghan has been reading food blogs since she was 13 yrs old. Growing up with her six siblings, she used to cook for them. Actually, her parents were at work and she started to cook for her brothers and sister.

They used to love her food and that appreciation from them motivate her to cook more. Her mom also helped her a lot and she started working on it.

Teaching herself photography, and learning about food and recipes she started her blog and morphed it into a business. She started making vlogs officially in 2012.

The growing business was the impetus for this dreamlike barn/kitchen/studio renovation. The Gerards were living in a rented house, and they started a search for a property that had a house and an apartment for Tieghan.

After the business’s success, they hired John Vietti of Vietti Construction for the renovation. No sooner, did she gains popularity with her wholesome recipes made with real ingredients and crazy stories of mountain life in a family of 9.

Tieghan has also partnered with Anthropologie, Land O’Lakes, and Whole Foods.

Taking about her books, she wrote Half Baked Harvest Super Simple: More Than 125 Recipes for Instant, Overnight, Meal-Prepped, and Easy Comfort Foods: A Cookbook in the year 2017, and likely Half Baked Harvest Super Simple: More Than 125 Recipes for Instant, Overnight, Meal-Prepped, and Easy Comfort Foods: A Cookbook in 2019.

Likely, the food channel has been featured on The Cooking Channel, Food Network, HGTV, Crate & Barrel, Shape Magazine, Self Magazine, the Huffington Post, and PopSugar, among others.
