Is Cody Rhodes related to Dusty Rhodes?

July 2024 Â· 3 minute read

Cody Rhodes, a prominent figure in the world of professional wrestling, is often associated with his legendary father, Dusty Rhodes.

This Fan Arch feature aims to delve into the familial connection between Cody and Dusty Rhodes, their shared professional wrestling careers, notable achievements, and any controversies that have surrounded them.

Familial Connection

Cody Rhodes, whose real name is Cody Garrett Runnels, was born on June 30, 1985, in Marietta, Georgia, to his parents, Dusty Rhodes and Michelle Rubio.

Dusty Rhodes, born as Virgil Riley Runnels Jr., was a widely celebrated professional wrestler, promoter, and trainer.

His impact on the wrestling industry is profound, and his legacy continues to influence the sport today.

Cody Rhodes is indeed related to Dusty Rhodes as his son.

The strong familial bond between the two has been a significant aspect of Cody's journey into professional wrestling, shaping his career and contributing to his success in the industry.

Shared Professional Wrestling Careers

Cody Rhodes followed in his father's footsteps, pursuing a career in professional wrestling.

He began his wrestling career in 2006 and went on to achieve significant success in various promotions, including WWE, Ring of Honor, and New Japan Pro-Wrestling.

Dusty Rhodes, on the other hand, had an illustrious career that spanned several decades.

Known for his captivating promos and in-ring abilities, Dusty became a three-time NWA World Heavyweight Champion and was inducted into the WWE Hall of Fame in 2007. 

Notable Achievements

Cody Rhodes

  • Two-time Intercontinental Champion in WWE
  • Multiple championships in other promotions, including ROH and Impact Wrestling
  • Founding member of All Elite Wrestling (AEW), where he has held the AEW TNT Championship and competed in high-profile matches

Dusty Rhodes

  • Three-time NWA World Heavyweight Champion
  • Inducted into the WWE Hall of Fame in 2007
  • Known for creating innovative and influential storylines and characters during his time as a booker and creative mind in wrestling promotions

Controversies

While both Cody and Dusty Rhodes have enjoyed immense success in their careers, controversies have also made their presence felt.

In the case of Dusty Rhodes, some controversies arose during his tenure as a booker and creative force in various wrestling promotions.

Disagreements over creative direction and backstage politics led to occasional friction with other wrestlers and management. 

As for Cody Rhodes, his career has been relatively free of major controversies. His focus on delivering compelling performances and his innovative approach to storytelling have garnered widespread acclaim from both fans and peers in the industry.

 

Ultimately, Cody Rhodes is unequivocally related to the legendary Dusty Rhodes as his son.

While Dusty Rhodes blazed a trail of success and innovation in his time, Cody Rhodes continues to build upon his family's illustrious wrestling heritage with his own remarkable achievements.
