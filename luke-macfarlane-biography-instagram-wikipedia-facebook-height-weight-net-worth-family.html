Thomas Luke Macfarlane is a Canadian-American actor and former singer. He is best known for his roles as Scotty Wandell on the ABC drama Brothers & Sisters, RAC Agent D’avin Jaqobis on the Space television science fiction series Killjoys, and the love interest in several Hallmark Channel movies.
