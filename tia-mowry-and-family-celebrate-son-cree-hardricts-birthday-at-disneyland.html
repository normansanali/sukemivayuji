TIA MOWRY AND FAMILY CELEBRATE SON CREE HARDRICTS BIRTHDAY AT DISNEYLAND

September 2024 · 1 minute read

Cree Hardrict celebrated his eighth birthday with his family at Disneyland this week. The son of Tia Mowry and Cory Hardrict had his aunt, uncles, and cousins to surround him with love at the Happiest Place On Earth.

Cree’s actual birthday is at the end of the month. The family, however, decided to commemorate his milestone a bit early. “@disneyland was everything,” Tia shared with her Instagram fans earlier this week. “The most magical place in the world,” she exclaimed. “@tameramowrytwo @tahj_mowry and myself used to live in #anaheim and we would hit and work on tv specials at @disneyland as kids. To bring our children and spend time as a family made it all so special.”

Cree got the royal treatment at his birthday celebration at Disneyland. Family and friends joined staff members in singing the Birthday Song to the youngster. Cree smiled from ear to ear while holding a cupcake with a candle on top. Just look at the excitement on his face!
