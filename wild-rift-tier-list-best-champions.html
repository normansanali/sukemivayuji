Best Champions to play in patch 4.1

September 2024 · 3 minute read

If you’re looking for a tier list of which Champions to use in Wild Rift for the current meta in patch 4.1, we’ve got you covered with everything you need to know for each lane.

Knowing which Champions are best suited to a current patch’s meta in Wild Rift is an essential part of helping you come out of a match victorious, and our tier list will show you exactly who the best-performing Champions in each role are.

So, let’s look at the best Wild Rift Champions in each role, and who you should pick when you next play.

Wild Rift tier list: Best Champions for each role

The below Champions are considered some of the best in the game’s meta right now, and will make for a solid pick for anyone who can master that champion.

Best Top lane Champions in Wild Rift

Top: Wukong, Renekton, Fiora, Garen, Jax, Gwen

Tank Champions are doing very well in the meta right now, so picking a Champion that can deal out lots of damage while simultaneously soaking up damage is an excellent strategy in the current patch.

This also means that Champions that do well in tank match-ups also thrive. Picks like Gwen and Fiora can go toe-to-toe with tanks and bruisers fairly easily, and Wukong is also a top pick for any player looking to win.

Best Jungle Champions in Wild Rift

Jungle: Gragas, Kha’Zix, Lee Sin, Wukong, Olaf

Junglers need to be able to clear the jungle quickly and help their laners. Champions like Gragas can do that easily, as they can support their team with crowd control and ganks.

Lee Sin is another great pick for higher-skilled players, while Champions like Kha’zix can take over a game if piloted well.

Best Mid lane Champions in Wild Rift

Mid: Ahri, Akali, Zoe, Vex, Yone, Karma, Diana

If you’re playing a mid-laner, you’re in luck, as there are many great picks for these Champions.

While the above options are all great choices, it’ll take a while to learn each match-up. Champions like Ahri and Karma are best suited for newer players, while more experienced players might want to learn Champions like Diana, Yone or Akali.

Wild Rift Tier List: Best Champions for the bot lane

The Bot lane is the only lane in Wild Rift where two players team up to work through the lane together. There aren’t as many ADCs as other roles, which makes dominant Champions even more commanding of Wild Rift’s tier list so far in 2023.

Best ADC Champions in Wild Rift

ADC: Vayne, Tristana, Lucian, Kai’sa, Samira

Vayne is an excellent option for an ADC right now due to the prominence of tankier Champions – she’s excellent at breaking down tanks quicker than other ADCs.

Deadlier picks include Champions Lucian and Samira, or if you want to try an all-rounder choose Tristana or Kai’sa. Either way, you can’t go wrong with these options right now.

Best Support Champions in Wild Rift

Support: Karma, Nami, Yuumi, Pyke, Janna

Enchanters are excellent choices in Wild Rift’s current meta as of patch 4.1. Paired with a Champion like Vayne, enchanters can constantly keep their carry alive when played correctly.

Furthermore, enchanters like Karma or Yuumi have solid damage in the lane, allowing you to survive the early stages easier. And if you want a more flashy pick, go for a playmaker like Pyke.
