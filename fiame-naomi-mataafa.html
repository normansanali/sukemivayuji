Fiame Naomi Mata’afa is a politician and High Chieftainess from Samoa. She is the daughter of Samoa’s first Prime Minister, Fiame Mata’afa Faumuina Mulinu’u II, and became the country’s first female Prime Minister in 2021. Mata’afa, the leader of Fa’atuatua I le Atua Samoa ua Tasi, was elected Prime Minister of Samoa by a majority vote in 2021. However, defeated Prime Minister Tuila’epa Sa’iele Malielegaoi refused to resign, precipitating the Samoan constitutional crisis in 2021. Mata’afa was appointed Prime Minister of Samoa by Samoa’s Court of Appeal on July 23, 2021.
