How Did Nikki Bella Pursue Her Career? Professional Life

After finishing her education, Nikki relocated to Los Angeles, where she began working odd jobs while attempting to land modeling or acting jobs. She appeared in a TV show called ‘Meet My Folks,’ where she was noticed by agents and began receiving offers to appear in commercials. She eventually appeared in a commercial with her sister.

World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) signed Nikki Bella and her twin sister Brie Bella to a developmental contract in June 2007. They were eventually assigned to Florida Championship Wrestling (FCW). They formed the duo ‘The Bella Twins,’ and she made her professional wrestling debut on September 15, 2007. In their debut match, they defeated Nattie Neidhart and Krissy Vaine, with Victoria Crawford as the special guest referee. Meanwhile, Nikki competed in some mixed tag team matches with Kofi Kingston and Robert Anthony. In 2008 and 2009, the duo continued to compete in tag team matches and grew in popularity.

On March 11, 2013, the Bella Twins made their return to WWE, facing and defeating The Funkadactyls. Nikki was given another chance to win the ‘WWE Diva championship’ in 2014. She won the title after defeating AJ Lee in a Survivor Series match, and she held it for nearly ten months. This was the longest-reigning WWE Diva champion ever. She defended the title against several opponents before losing it to Charlotte in a ‘Night of Champions’ match. She then took a break from wrestling due to neck injuries.
