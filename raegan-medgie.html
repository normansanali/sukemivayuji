Raegan Medgie FOX 5, New York

Medgie is an award-winning reporter who currently serves as a reporter, anchor and forecaster at FOX 5 News Team after joining the station in 2017. Before joining FOX 5, she forecasted the weather at The Weather Channel. She started her on-air career for the first time while at the launch of AMHQ with Sam Champion. As the Northeast Correspondent based in New York, she became part of “Wake Up With Al” and MSNBC as she crisscrossed the country, chasing storms and humanizing the aftermath.

Raegan also worked as a general assignment reporter covering breaking news for WCBS/CBS2 New York. Medgie also filled in as the entertainment reporter for CBS-FM. Before making New York home, she was a reporter and anchor for WNEP (ABC) Newswatch 16 in Scranton, Pennsylvania; WETM (NBC) in Elmira, New York where she earned an Associated Press Award for Best Enterprise Reporting; and TV2 News in Allentown, Pennsylvania. Her first taste as a reporter began at Temple Update, the student-run TV station at her alma mater Temple University in Philadelphia
