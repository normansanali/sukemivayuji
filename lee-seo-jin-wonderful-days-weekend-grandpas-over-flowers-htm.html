Fill Your Weekend With Lee Seo Jin: Wonderful Days and Grandpas Over Flowers 2

August 2024 Â· 2 minute read

Actor Lee Seo Jin will dominate weekend television.

Lee Seo Jin is currently acting in KBS drama "Wonderful Days" as a cold-hearted prosecutor who has closed off from his family. The drama has already reached viewer ratings of more than 30% after its first two episodes.

Lee Seo Jin plays an important character who slowly warms his heart to his family. His charismatic and cynical portrayal of the role is regarded highly.

Lee Seo Jin will also continue to make an appearance in tvN's "Grandpas Over Flowers 2". In season 2 of the popular show "Grandpas Over Flowers", he will once again be a porter-slave in Spain.

It is expected that Lee Seo Jin will show contrasting sides as a cold-hearted prosecutor in the drama and a faulty but kind porter on the show.

In season 1, Lee Seo Jin cared and slaved for his acting seniors Lee Soon Jae, Shin Gu, Park Geun Hyung, and Baek Il Sub. Fans cheered as Lee Seo Jin grudgingly complied with the delicate caring of his seniors.

During the drama's press conference, Lee Seo Jin stated that he visited Spain for "Grandpas Over Flowers 2" during filming of the drama: "Unfailingly again, I experienced a mental breakdown. I'm at the age where I need a porter myself. I don't know if I'll go again if asked a third time".

In regards to the contrasting characters to be aired, he stated: "I'm worried because I'm showing two sides that are completely different. I don't know whether viewers can adjust".

Meanwhile, "Wonderful Days" airs at 7:55 PM on weekends and "Grandpas Over Flowers 2" at 9:50 PM every Friday starting March 7th.
