How Is Uncle Owen Related to Luke Skywalker? Fans and viewers of Star Wars are eager to learn how Uncle Owen is related to Luke Skywalker in the series. This article explains their relationship in greater detail.

The plot of Star Wars begins on Tatooine, where Luke Skywalker lives on a farm with his aunt and uncle. He is eager to leave this world and join his friends at the Academy. Meanwhile, a tyrant has taken control of the galaxy and constructed a powerful “Death Star” capable of annihilating entire worlds.

The film was mainly directed and written by George Lucas, has 63 accumulated awards, and was nominated for 29 titles. It has also won six Oscar awards.

According to the answer found in Quora, Uncle Owen’ is not a blood relative of Luke’s.

Owen Lars’ father, Cliegg Lars, was Shmi Skywalker’s spouse.

She was Anakin Skywalker’s slave mother, as in the film. She mysteriously became pregnant with him (Anakin has no biological father).

Cliegg found Shmi, purchased her from Watto, later released, fell in love with, and married her when Anakin was training as a Jedi Padawan.

As a result, Cliegg is Anakin’s and Luke’s Stepfather and Step-Grandfather. Following that, Owen is Anakin’s Stepbrother, making him Luke’s Step-Uncle.
