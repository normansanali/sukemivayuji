When Does Joseline Cabaret New York Come On?

Joseline Cabaret New York comes on 8pm EST or 5 pm PST, every Sunday on Zeus. The first episode premiered on July 23.

People are anticipating the new season after the season finale of Joseline’s Cabaret: Las Vegas. The season last aired in March, and the reunion episode dropped on May 2022.

After six months, Ms. Wet Wet shared about Joseline Cabaret Season 4 Roll Call on December 23, 2022.

The one-night audition for the Joselines Cabaret New York Season 4 was held on June 5, 2023. The chosen ladies move into Joselines Cabaret House on June 6, 2023.

Finally, the Zeus Network dropped the season 4 teaser on July 3, 2023, introducing Joseline Hernandez as a new woman.

She added this season, the audience can witness the new life of Joseline, which will be different from all three seasons.

At the end of the teaser, it added the new season will premier this July.

Finally, the official trailer has released on July 17, 2023, where the executive producer added there was no better place than New York to showcase her talent.

The network even announced the Joseline Cabaret Season 4 release date is July 23.

After seven days, the premier episode finally dropped on Zeus on Sunday after airing the season finale of Bad Boys Texas and the new episode of Crazy In Love Season 2.

Like the other three seasons, it may run for three months until October.

Joseline got emotional during the premiere episode of Joselines Cabaret New York for her One Night Only performance. She even opened up about performing for the first time sober with no coke, no ecstasy or pills.

The episode of Joseline Cabaret is streaming on Zeus Network. You can subscribe for only $5.99/mo or $59.99/year.

Joseline Cabaret Past Winners

Chanel Tso is the only winner of Joseline Cabaret.

Among the three previous seasons, in only one season, the contestant received the prize money of $10,000 alongside the opportunity for being the next season’s cast member.

The first season, Joseline’s Cabaret: Miami, shared the daily life struggle of the executive producer Hernandez. Daisy, Ms. JaaDreams, Lucky Husrla, Sapphire, and Chazzity joined as the dancer.

In the next season, Joseline launched a cabaret show in Atlanta, Georgia. This season introduced a new concept as it presented a reality competition element.

Joseline introduced ten girls and rewarded the girls with the captain title. While the girls Lexi Blow, Natural, Lucky, and Chanel performed with the executive producer in the cabaret, Chanel was the overall winner.

Chanel won Joseline’s Cabaret: Atlanta and received $10,000 and the chance to complete the cabaret in Vegas.

Afterward, ten new cast members join Joseline’s Cabaret: Las Vegas alongside Captain Chanel and Lexi Blow.

Amber from Joseline Cabaret was eliminated in episode 10.

Lollipop from Joseline Cabaret featured in three episodes and finally became the cabaret dancer.
