Tyrel Jackson Williams (born March 16, 1997) is an American teen actor, singer and musician. He currently stars as Leo Francis Dooley in the Disney XD series, Lab Rats. Williams was previously seen in Everybody Hates Chris, as a younger version of Chris, played by his older brother, Tyler James Williams.
