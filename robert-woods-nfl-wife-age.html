Robert Woods is an American football wide receiver currently playing for the Los Angeles Rams of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football at the University of Southern California from 2010 to 2012.

Robert Woods Bio; Parents, Education, Height, Weight

Robert Thomas Woods was born on 10 April 1992 to parents Robert Jr. and Sharon Woods. He grew up with a sister Olivia woods in Carson, California.

Woods pursued his early education at Junípero Serra High School in Gardena, California. He subsequently got a scholarship to attend the University of Southern California. He stands at a height of 6 feet (1.83 meters) and weighs 195 lbs (88 kg).

His Career; NFL, Stats

Robert played for the USC Trojans football for three seasons from 2010 to 2012. Afterward, he was drafted by the Buffalo Bills in the second round (41st overall) of the 2013 NFL Draft.

Robert Woods’ NFL career stats as of 2019 (Picture: Pro-Football-Reference)

Woods made his professional regular-season debut and first NFL start in the season-opener against the New England Patriots. In his second season, he had a career-high 65 receptions for 699 receiving yards and five touchdowns in 16 games and 15 starts. He played for the Bills for four seasons and was signed by the Los Angeles Rams in March 2017. He had an 11–5 record, clinching the NFC West in the 2017 season.

Contract & Salary

Robert signed a 5-year contract with the Los Angeles Rams, worth $34,000,000 on 9 March 2017. It includes $15,000,000 guaranteed and an average annual salary of $6,800,000.

Who Is Robert Woods’ Wife?

The NFL player is currently married to a charming lady named Alexandra Barbee. The couple exchanged the wedding vows on 7 July 2018 after together for several years.

  • Robert lost his sister Olivia due to cancer on 19 April 2007. He even honored her in his commencement speech upon college graduation.
